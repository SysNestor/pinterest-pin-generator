Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-14

This application is a personal/internal tool used to generate Pinterest pin metadata and (after you connect your Pinterest account via OAuth) publish pins to boards you select.

Data we collect

  • Pinterest OAuth tokens (access token and, if provided by Pinterest, refresh token) used to list your boards and publish pins on your behalf.
  • Content you provide or generate, such as product text, pin titles, descriptions, hashtags, and the destination link you enter.
  • Images you upload for publishing pins.

How we use your data

  • To provide core functionality: generate pin plans, list boards, and publish pins you explicitly request.
  • To communicate with Pinterest APIs as required to publish content.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is shared only with Pinterest (and only when needed) to complete the actions you request, such as listing boards and publishing pins.

Data retention

Tokens and content may be stored locally for the app to function. You can disconnect the Pinterest integration to stop future publishing.

Security

Keep your API credentials private. Do not share tokens or secret keys publicly.
